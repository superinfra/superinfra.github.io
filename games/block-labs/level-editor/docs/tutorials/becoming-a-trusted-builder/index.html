Becoming a Trusted Builder

Trusted Builders (TBs) have access to special perks across the level editor, game, and Discord server.

Becoming a Trusted Builder
Sections: 2
Skill level: Intermediate
Length: ~5 min.

1. What is a Trusted Builder? (2 min.)

Trusted Builder is a special rank that you earn by simply creating and submitting a level that makes it in-game!

There are a few variations of this rank, each with different perks:

Rank Requirements Perks
Trusted Builder (TB) 1 accepted submission Text scripting permissions, multiplayer title, Discord role
Extra Trusted Builder (ETB) 5 submissions, 2 accepted submissions Exclusive block, Discord level 50, new multiplayer title, new Discord role
Legendary Builder (LB/LTB) Rewarded by owner, no specific requirements Free gamepass of choice, custom block skin, custom multiplayer title, new Discord role

2. Why didn't I earn my rank? (2 min.)

You should automatically get your rank if you earn it, but if you didn't, please tell us by opening a ticket in #tickets (select "Something else") and we will try to resolve the issue.
